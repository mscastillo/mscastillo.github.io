Welcome

Hi, this is the academic-research website of Manuel Sánchez Castillo.

About me...

I was born in La Línea de la Concepción (Spain) in July, 30th 1981.

I moved to Granada when I was 18 y.o. to start my universitary studies in Physics.

I got my PhD degree in the Physics and Space Sciences program at the University of Granada on 2012.

After two years as a postdoct in the Haematopoietic Stem Cell Laboratory of the University of Cambridge I joined to the Computational Biology group of a biotechnology company in April 2015.
